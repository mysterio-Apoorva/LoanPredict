Under the Hood

Introduction

How LoanPredict transforms raw applicant data into a reliable loan-eligibility decision.

The Problem

Traditional loan processing relies heavily on manual review, making it slow, inconsistent, and prone to human bias. LoanPredict replaces that bottleneck with a data-driven ML pipeline that delivers consistent, objective predictions in under a second.

Dataset

The model was trained on the widely-used Loan Prediction dataset containing records of previous applicants with features like income, credit history, education, and property area — labels indicate whether the loan was ultimately approved or rejected.

Data Engineering

Missing values are dropped, categorical variables are numerically encoded, and continuous features are normalised with StandardScaler — the same scaler is persisted and reused at inference to prevent data leakage.

Model Selection

Three classifiers are evaluated on a held-out test set: Logistic Regression, Decision Tree Classifier, and K-Nearest Neighbours. The best-performing model is serialised to model.pkl and loaded at startup — no retraining needed at inference time.

Serving the Prediction

The Flask app receives the form POST, applies identical preprocessing, passes the scaled vector to the loaded model, and returns the binary decision along with the classifier's confidence score.
